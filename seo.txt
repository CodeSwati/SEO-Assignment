Q1) What is SEO and explain the importance of SEO?
ANS) Search engine optimization is the process of improving the quality and quantity of website traffic to a website or a
 web page from search engines.

Importance: The more effective SEO efforts, the higher webpage ranking and the better webpage visibility.
